Got a Question?

We have some answers

  • What is RentBase?
    RentBase is a platform built by rental agents to help you qualify and manage the 3 L’s of your rental business; Landlords, Listings, and Leads.

    We provide you with desktop and mobile tools, stay on track of all your rental deals and follow ups, and never have a potential deal fall through the cracks again.
  • How does it work?
    Instead of keeping your contacts and leads in one place, then separately storing your listings in another place; RentBase is an all-in-one platform to house (no pun intended) your entire rental business in one place.

    You can easily import your listings and contacts into RentBase and provide an easy-to-use database to connect and manage all aspects of your business (landlords, their listings, and your leads).

    Leads are automatically qualified and filtered into RentBase and matched with the listings that can best lead to a deal.

    Landlord follow-ups are tracked and updated as soon as you place a call, text or email them and you can easily create follow-ups and tasks per lead, listing or landlord.
  • How do I sign up for it?
    To get our newest updates and apply for access to our Beta product, enter your email here to request access.

    If you don’t see the email within 2 min it’s probably stuck in your spam/junk folder.
  • What does the Beta product do?
    The Beta product is a little taste of what RentBase has to offer. Instead of chasing down a new lead and trying to connect on the phone or back and forth in emails, you can provide them your personalized RentBase link to qualify your leads via their phone or desktop.

    As soon as a lead completes your form, you’ll receive:

    A text notifying you the lead has completed the form.

    An email with all the answers.

    An excel sheet that will update automatically every time a lead is added.

  • Who is this product for?
    Any real estate agent or property manager who wants to save time and manage their rental listings and leads more efficiently. You don’t need to be renting 60+ apartments to get value from us. Even one good listing can attract 50-100 unique leads and RentBase can save you hours of time on them.
  • How much will it cost?
    Right now our Beta is being offered for free. We’d just like to hear your feedback on it. When we launch in the Spring we’ll have an early adopter monthly rate of $15 a month.
  • Does it work on my phone?
    Yes it does! We know how much agents are on the go and we’re making RentBase adapt to your needs. Anytime you’re using it from a desktop, it is updating your mobile device and vice-versa.
  • Will my leads or listing's information be shared with 3rd parties?
    No.

    We know how important the data you collect or share on RentBase is to you and we take that VERY seriously. Our technology has been designed using the most up-to-date protections and security measures out there. Our platform’s architect comes from an elite military software unit and has implemented the same approach to protecting your information. We do not sell or advertise your information or your leads data to any 3rd parties, nor do we share or sell information obtained in RentBase to users within RentBase.
  • Is there a limit to how many leads or listings I can work with?
    No, there’s no limit. As long as you have 1 listing in the system, you can have 1000 or more. Same goes for your leads.
  • How will this help my rental business?
    One of the first things we offer you is your time back! RentBase can automate the most time consuming and tedious aspects of your business and free you up to close more deals and make more money!

    We help you avoid time-wasting leads just as much as we help you find highly qualified leads. Furthermore your leads will appreciate this approach that saves them time as well. We know, we’ve asked and the results are overwhelming!
  • Does this work for sales too?
    It probably could but RentBase is tailor-made to support your rental business. We know a rental agent’s needs and the rental-cycle differ greatly from their sales side. While there are countless CRM and lead management products out there, there’s only one platform built specifically to cover your A-Z rental business.
