Got a Question?

We have some answers

  • What is RentBase?
    We provide you with desktop and mobile tools to stay on track of all your rental deals and follow ups, and never have a potential deal fall through the cracks again.
  • How does it work?
    RentBase is an all-in-one platform to house (no pun intended) your entire rental business in one place.

    We provide you an easy-to-use platform to upload all your contacts, listings, and photos into RentBase. Once you’re onboarded, we help you manage all aspects of your business (landlords, their listings, and your leads).

    Leads are automatically qualified and filtered into RentBase, then matched with the listings that can best lead to a deal.

    Landlord follow-ups are tracked and updated as soon as you place a call, text or email them and you can seamlessly create follow-ups and tasks per lead, listing or landlord.
  • How do I sign up for it?
    To get our newest updates and apply for access to our Beta product, enter your email here to request access.

    If you don’t see the email within 2 min it’s probably stuck in your spam/junk folder.
  • What does the Beta product do?
    The Beta product is just a taste of what RentBase has to offer. Instead of chasing down new leads and trying to get them on the phone, or waiting for them to respond to your qualifying questions in emails, we create a personalized RentBase link to send your leads.

    Leads tend to complete the form within 10-15 min at nearly a 90% conversion rate. Once completed you’ll receive notification that a leads has completed the form and all that lead will be uploaded into your database
  • Who is this product for?
    Any real estate agent or property manager looking to save time and manage their rental listings and leads more efficiently.

    You don’t need to rent 60+ apartments in order to see the value of RentBase. Even with one good listing you can attract 50-100 unique leads whichRentBase can then help save you hours of time qualifying.
  • How much will it cost?
    Right now our Beta is being offered for free. We’d just like to hear your feedback on it. When we launch in the Spring we’ll have an early adopter monthly rate of $15 a month.
  • Does it work on my phone?
    Yes it does! We know how much agents are on the go and we’re making RentBase adapt to your needs. Anytime you’re using it from a desktop, it is updating your mobile device and vice-versa.
  • Will my leads or listing's information be shared with 3rd parties?
    No.

    We know the sensitive data you’re collecting is very important and we take that VERY seriously anytime you input it on RentBase. Our technology has been designed using the most up-to-date protections and security measures out there. Our platform’s architect comes from an elite military software unit and has implemented the same approach to protecting your information.

    We do not sell or advertise your information, or your leads data to any 3rd parties, nor do we share or sell information obtained in RentBase to users within RentBase.
  • Is there a limit to how many leads or listings I can work with?
    No, there’s no limit. As long as you have 1 listing in the system, you can have 1000 or more. Same goes for your leads.
  • How will this help my rental business?
    One of the first things we offer you is your time back! RentBase can automate the most time consuming and tedious aspects of your business and free you up to close more deals and make more money!

    We help you avoid time-wasting leads just as much as we help you find highly qualified leads. Furthermore your leads will appreciate this approach that saves them time as well. We know, we’ve asked and the results are overwhelming!
  • Does this work for sales too?
    It probably can but RentBase is tailor-made to support your rental business. We know a rental agent’s needs and their struggles, plus the rental-cycle differs greatly from their sales side.

    While there are countless CRM and lead management products out there supporting sales agents, there’s only one platform built specifically to cover your A-Z rental business.
